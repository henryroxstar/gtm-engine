Company-specific social-media tuning, read by the content skills **alongside** the generic
platform playbooks in `docs/{linkedin,instagram,x,facebook}-optimization.md`. The generic
*method* stays in those docs; everything company-specific lives here (tenant boundary).

Your wedge, content pillars and posting handle live in `PROFILE.md` — don't restate them here.
This file is for the per-platform tuning the generic playbooks can't know.

## Two clocks  *(delete if you have one region)*
When your audience straddles two primary regions, don't average them into a mushy middle —
run two slots and tag each piece to the region it's for. Name the regions and their prime
posting windows, e.g. `APAC (evenings local)` + `US (mid-morning ET)`.

## Voices bench
Who posts, and in which register. E.g. founder (visionary POV), engineering/DevRel (builder
explainers), a policy translator, an employee-advocate bench.

## Per-platform cheat sheets

### LinkedIn
- **Lead format:** <e.g. carousels for explainers/frameworks; founder video for POV>
- **Never:** <e.g. links in body, hashtag stacks, engagement bait, pods>

### X
- **Lead with:** <e.g. replies for growth + threads for authority + reactive takes>
- **Never:** <e.g. outbound links in the main post, hashtag stacks, rage-bait>

### Instagram
- **Role:** <e.g. awareness + brand + recruiting — not the conversion channel>
- **Cadence:** <e.g. ~2 Reels + 3–5 feed posts/week>
- **Never:** <e.g. text-heavy carousels, unbranded visuals>

### Facebook
- **Role:** <e.g. community + longer-form explainers>
- **Never:** <e.g. cross-posting LinkedIn copy verbatim>
