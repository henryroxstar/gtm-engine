# Your source inbox — drop raw material here

This folder is where **you hand over raw material** for your GTM engine to learn from. Anything that
helps it sound like you and stay true to your facts belongs here:

- Company / product background (decks, one-pagers, website copy, FAQs)
- Case studies, customer stories, testimonials
- How you write — a voice guide, or just a few of your own emails/posts as samples
- For the industries you sell into: the **legendary voices** you respect (analysts, operators,
  regulators), and the blogs, articles, quotes, news, and regulations that shape how those buyers
  think and speak

## How to add material

Whatever is easiest — you don't need to format it:

1. **Paste** it into chat and say "add this to my knowledge."
2. **Point** the assistant at a file or folder on your machine.
3. **Drop** documents straight into this folder (PDF, docx, md, txt — the assistant converts and reads them).

The assistant then **condenses** what you drop here into your structured knowledge pack
(`../company.md`, `../product.md`, `../case-studies.md`, `../voice.md`, the `../industry/` packs, the
`../adversary-testing/` voices, …). Raw files stay here as the provenance trail; the condensed
topics are what the skills read at runtime.

## Notes

- **This is raw input, not a runtime source.** Files here are deliberately excluded from the
  freshness/usage machinery — they're your working material, condensed *into* the managed topics,
  not read directly by drafting skills.
- **More material = sharper drafts**, but you can start with almost nothing and add over time.
- Nothing here leaves your machine. It's your private material, stored with your profile.
