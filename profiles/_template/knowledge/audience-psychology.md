# Audience psychology

The psychological layer under `icp-personas.md`. Personas say *who the buyer is and what they need*
(Pain·Claim·Gain); this file captures *what they feel, believe, and fear* — and filters it for what this
company can credibly say.

**Build this with the method:** [`docs/audience-psychology-method.md`](../../../docs/audience-psychology-method.md).
`profile-onboard` scaffolds this skeleton; fill it from the real `icp-personas.md`, `company.md`, and
`case-studies.md`. **Anchor every line to a source — never invent an emotion.** Where the source is thin,
write "thin — needs a real customer conversation" rather than guessing.

Read by `content-plan` (to shape `brief.angle` + apply the founder-fit tag at Gate 1) and `builder-studio`
Step 0 (to aim each asset).

## Per-persona block (copy one per persona in icp-personas.md)

### <Persona name / title>
- **Emotional stakes:** <what they fear, usually reputational/personal — anchored to role + company.md>
- **Believed but never said:** <the private conviction they won't state out loud>
- **Contrarian thesis that reframes them:** <the company's non-obvious belief, drawn from company.md>
- **Reasoning style:** control-trust | cost-benefit | speed-risk
- **Proof as belief-shift:** <the customer's change of mind, from case-studies.md — or "thin — needs a real story">
- **Founder-fit angles:**
  - Strong: <angle the company can own as the expert>
  - Partial (constraint: <…>): <usable with care>
  - Do-not-drive: <true about the audience but off-positioning / beyond credible expertise>

---

## Founder-fit filter (quick reference)

Tag each angle by running it through: **Expertise** (can we speak as the expert?) · **Understanding**
(does it prove we get this buyer?) · **Value** (is the reader better off pre-pitch?) · **Association**
(does owning it reinforce our real positioning?). Strong = all four · Partial = usable with a stated
constraint · Do-not-drive = record it so the writer isn't tempted. This is where complementary-positioning,
verify-against-canonical-refs, and the profile's cert posture become operational.
